Interests

Composition/Guitar

Thanks to my brother who was always introducing me to new artists, and always promoted discovery without discrimination when I was younger,
I've developped a true passion for music in general.

In my early teenage years, I got an intense urge to create it. And after a couple months ,I managed to convince my parents to buy me my first instrument
A very cheap, and awful to play acoustic guitar that I loved very much.

Music Production

Ever since i got my first guitar, I've always wanted to create and record my own music, and with studios being completly out of my reach, I went to the next best thing:
doing it all by myself and recording everything using free software and my phone's microphone.

I have since moved on to more professional software like ableton but I still remember the struggle I went through just to create a couple cool sounds and scream my feelings out.

Computer Science

I've always found computers interesting whilst growing up,
whether it was the inner workings of video game engines, or in more recent years, the way these modern machines are built.
I remember always being considered the tech kid that others could ask for help.

Needless to say, I don't think I've surprised anyone with my study choices.
